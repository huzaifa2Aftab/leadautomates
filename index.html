The Real Problem

Most leads are not lost because the offer is bad.

They are lost because the response is slow, the follow-up is manual, and the team gets busy before the lead is properly handled.

Slow first reply

When a lead waits too long, they usually contact another business before your team gets back to them.

Missed calls

Unanswered calls often come from high-intent buyers. A simple recovery text can bring them back.

Weak follow-up

Most leads need more than one message. Automation keeps the conversation moving without chasing manually.
