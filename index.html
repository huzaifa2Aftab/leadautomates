FAQ

Common questions before starting.

A few simple answers about how the automation setup usually works.

Which tools can you work with?

We can build systems with GoHighLevel, n8n, Make, Zapier, Airtable, Google Sheets, WhatsApp, Twilio, Vapi, Retell, Calendly, Google Calendar, and other CRM or API-based tools.

Do I need a CRM before starting?

No. If you already have a CRM, we can connect it. If you do not, we can recommend a simple setup based on your business and lead volume.

Can this work for missed calls?

Yes. Missed-call recovery can send an automatic text-back, notify your team, create or update the lead, and trigger follow-up if the lead does not reply.

Can you add AI voice agents?

Yes. AI voice agents can help with inbound calls, lead qualification, basic FAQs, reminders, and appointment booking when the use case is clear.
